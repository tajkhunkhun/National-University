How we help ANDers?

National University offers a holistic approach to student support, well-being, and success called “Whole Human Education™.”

Financial Support

1:1 assistance for navigating the financial aid process, generous transfer credit policy, needs-based tuition reduction grants, and scholarships.

Flexible Academics

4-, 8-, and 12-week online classes, unlimited free tutoring, individual coaching, writing center, and 150+ academic degree programs, credentials, and certificates.

Career Mentoring

NU Mentoring Network for individual student and alumni career guidance, professional development opportunities, access to job listings, and customized career support.

Wellness Focussed

Free virtual mental health counseling, access to wellness resources, and Student Wellness Center offering 1:1 support for life challenges.

Valuing Community

Web-based student networking platform, student clubs and community service events, flexible online options to balance work and family.

Become Inspired by Our Original Anthem The Whole You

This song honors the strength and determination of NU’s “ANDers”— students pursuing higher education while managing work, parenting, family caregiving, military service, and more.
